Hello and welcome to Re-Story, a sharing space for all creative writing.
Here on Re-Story you are not restricted on what you want to read or write. Whether it is creative writing, a blog post you really want to share, something personal you want to get off your chest, or even homework, Re-Story allows you to do just that as well as being able to share you're writing to the public. You can state who you are or go anonymous to post your body of work. Feel free to hit the Read table to check out what has been posted recenetly, or hit Create and post your own.
